# GraphRAG and QA Results

GraphRAG and QA results should be presented with extra care because they combine retrieval quality with answer quality. If the graph module or generation layer is not fully stable yet, the report should not force a final quantitative story. Instead, it can show prototype examples, qualitative observations, or partial results with clear labels.

When this part is complete, the most useful evidence will be grounded answer examples, citation correctness, and failure cases where the system either misses the right evidence or produces an unsupported answer. Those examples will help show whether the full application is ready for practical use.
