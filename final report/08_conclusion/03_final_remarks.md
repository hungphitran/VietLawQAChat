# Final Remarks

The project shows how a retrieval-first design can support legal question answering in Vietnamese. By combining sparse retrieval, dense retrieval, reranking, and an application-oriented system layer, it creates a practical base for a grounded legal assistant.

The next step is to finish the remaining system pieces and tighten the evaluation of the final end-to-end pipeline. Once that is done, the project will not only be a retrieval benchmark study, but also a usable legal QA system with clearer real-world value.
